## Problem

Loi N° 2010/021 (cybersécurité et protection des données) establishes data-protection framework. Personal data (names, IDs from RCCM, NIU) requires lawful basis. The architect declares processing to ANTIC if processing personal data at scale; thresholds need 2026 verification with counsel. EXEC §16.2 admits the requirement; no plan to comply.

## Fix

**Pre-Phase-1 declaration** drafted by counsel within 30 days of M0a. Cited in MVP §09 instead of (or alongside) 'GDPR audit'. Decision-log entry on submission and on response.
