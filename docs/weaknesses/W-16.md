## Problem

ECE < 5% requires ≥ 30 ground-truth-labelled cases. These come from solo archive research before the system exists — hundreds of solo hours over 12 weeks per EXEC §21.4.

## Fix

**60-day shadow mode at M2 exit**. System produces findings to a private queue with no alerts and no anchoring. Architect labels in parallel during M3-M4. ECE is computed from shadow findings + historical seed, weighted equally. The §20 thirty-case minimum becomes a *floor*, not a *ceiling*.

This preserves the SRD §19.5 ECE < 5% acceptance criterion while making the timeline deliverable.
