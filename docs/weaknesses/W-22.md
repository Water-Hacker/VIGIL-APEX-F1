## Problem

SRD §05 / EXEC §39 give generic Tier 1/2/3 threat model. Do not address ENEO load-shedding as availability attack, contractor-funded SIM-cloning attacks on the architect, BEAC-route financial pressure on the SAS, etc.

## Fix

**THREAT-MODEL-CMR.md** extends SRD §05 with 8 actor classes and 7 Cameroon-specific TTPs. Reviewed quarterly by architect, annually with technical pillar.
