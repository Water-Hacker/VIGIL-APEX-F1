## Problem

EXEC §04 says 8 keys; SRD §17.2 says '5 at M0c + 3 at M3 = 8'; HSK is written for a 'pair' of 2 keys and treats single-architect ↔ polygon-signer as the only relationship. The HSK 'pair' model is the operational outlier — never reconciled with the 5-pillar council.

## Fix

**Adopt 8 keys total** (EXEC §04 wins). HSK is to be rewritten as the **YubiKey Estate Manual** covering all 8 (5 pillar + architect primary + polygon-signer + spare). The 'pair' semantics survives only as the architect↔polygon-signer relationship within Section 4 of the rewritten HSK.

Proposal includes a 9th deep-cold key per W-08 fix.
